Common Questions

Everything you need to know before we get started.

We typically work with brands spending at least $5,000/month on ads. This gives us enough data to optimize quickly and enough room to scale profitably. If you're spending less, we can still help with email/SMS and CRO.

Most clients see measurable improvements within the first 30 days — better ROAS, lower CPAs, or increased email revenue. Significant scaling typically happens in months 2-3 as we dial in the winning creative and audiences.

We offer month-to-month agreements after an initial 90-day onboarding period. The 90 days gives us enough time to build your strategy, launch campaigns, and prove results. After that, you stay because it's working — not because you're locked in.

We specialize in Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram), Google Ads (Search, Shopping & PMax), TikTok Ads, Klaviyo (email & SMS), and conversion rate optimization. We also work with Pinterest and Snapchat for specific verticals.

Our growth audit is a comprehensive review of your current ad accounts, email/SMS setup, website conversion funnel, and competitive positioning. You'll get a custom report with specific, actionable recommendations — whether you work with us or not.

We offer flat-fee monthly retainers based on the scope of services. No hidden fees, no percentage of ad spend markups. You'll know exactly what you're paying before we start. We'll walk through pricing on our strategy call.
